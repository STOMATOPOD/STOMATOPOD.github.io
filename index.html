Food Finder Problem Statement

Finding food that is tasty, healthy, and affordable is difficult for college students. Restaurants with central locations on campus often charge more than ones even a small distance away. Convenient restaurants also tend to serve food that is unhealthy to eat regularly, like burgers, bagels, or breakfast biscuits. Our solution should provide a way for students to find food that is delicious, healthy, and affordable.
